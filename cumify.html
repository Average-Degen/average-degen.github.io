Settings

The settings that you use are very important when cumifying
They determine how much cum to add and how much to change the body when adding cum

Setting Value Explanation
Masked content original This has to be original otherwise the result will not include the face
CFG Scale 7 ‐ 12 Determines how much weight to put on the prompt and hence how much cum to add to the image
Denoise strength 0.2 ‐ 0.5 Determines much to change from the original image. A value of 1 would probably be just cum with no visible face
